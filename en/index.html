Who are we?

Afrit Ltd. is a consulting company that offers comprehensive services in the field of project development and management. Additionally, we provide public procurement consultations, development of strategic documents, as well as business planning and analysis.
Our clients are business entities, non-governmental organizations, as well as government and municipal administrations. We have been working together for six years. We don't aim to convince you that we are "one of the leading consulting companies in Bulgaria," but we have the confidence to assert that we are good at what we do.
